Welcome to Prospero Barn

Prospero Barn is divided into 2 cafe areas, namely the indoor and outdoor sections which have a large area. When you first enter this cafe, you will be greeted by a building that has a modern-minimalist concept but has a distinctive touch of European buildings in it. This cafe is far from the downtown area, so you can feel a conducive and calm atmosphere when you are at this cafe.
